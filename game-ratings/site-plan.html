Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to deliver clear, informative, and unbiased reviews of video games across multiple platforms. It helps users quickly understand a game's quality, content, and suitability based on gameplay, graphics, story, and overall value.

Audience

Our intended audience is gamers or people who may be intrested about quickly learning about and it's quality

Dynamic elements

I will use JavaScript to add dynamic elements to my site by enabling interactive features such as sorting and filtering game reviews by genre, platform, or rating. Additionally, JavaScript will handle user interactions like expanding review summaries and displaying review scores with visual elements like progress bars or star ratings.
